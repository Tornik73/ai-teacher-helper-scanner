Privacy Policy for "AI Scanner" Chrome Extension

Last Updated: February 3, 2026
Effective Date: February 3, 2026

1. Overview

"AI Scanner" is a Chrome extension designed to help teachers and students export flashcards from Quizlet to Wordwall. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Data We Collect

This extension collects only the following data:

  • Flashcard Content: Terms and definitions from Quizlet pages (only when you explicitly click the export button)
  • Session Keys: Temporary identifiers used to transfer data between tabs (automatically deleted after export)

Data We DO NOT Collect

  • ❌ Personal information (name, email, address, phone number)
  • ❌ Authentication credentials (passwords, usernames, tokens)
  • ❌ Location data (GPS, IP address)
  • ❌ Browsing history
  • ❌ Cookies or tracking data
  • ❌ Payment information
  • ❌ Medical or health information

3. How We Use Your Data

Collected flashcard data is used exclusively for:

  • Extracting flashcard content from Quizlet pages
  • Transferring flashcard data to Wordwall for creating educational games
  • Displaying flashcards in the extension popup for preview

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Storage Location: All data is stored locally in your browser's chrome.storage.local
  • Data Transmission: Flashcard data is transferred only between tabs within your browser
  • Data Deletion: Session data is automatically deleted after the export process completes
  • No Server Storage: We do NOT send data to external servers or cloud services
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Flashcard data is never shared with third parties

5. Permissions Used

The extension requests the following permissions:

Permission Purpose
activeTab Detect Quizlet pages to extract flashcard content
scripting Inject scripts to extract and process flashcard data
storage Store temporary flashcard data during export
https://quizlet.com/* Access Quizlet pages to read flashcards
https://wordwall.net/* Transfer data to Wordwall for import

6. User Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • ✓ No Tracking: The extension does not track your activity
  • ✓ Manual Operation: Export only happens when you explicitly click the button
  • ✓ Local Storage: All data remains on your device unless you manually export
  • ✓ No Automatic Collection: The extension does not automatically collect data
  • ✓ Delete Anytime: Close the extension to stop all data collection

7. Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date.
